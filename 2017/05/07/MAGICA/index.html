MAGICA!

Posted on 2017-05-07

I’ve been a Dota 2 player for almost 10 years and I love playing mooba games. Recently, I began to have an idea to develop my own moba game. Doesn’t that sound cool?

I want to design my own charaters and enpower them with cool magic skills!
I want to design a unique game mechanism that is never seen in the existing moba game!
I want to have fun in the whole development process and this is the most important thing!

Fisrt, I drew two cute girls and used them as the game characters with super powerful skill. This is not too hard for me. I have a lot of 3D experience, so voxel modeling is relatively easy for sure.
For now, the game only has two characters. The left girl is Naoko. She’s a witch, using a staff. The right one is Yuri, she a warrior, wielding a long sword. Naoko is capable of firing magic balls to attack and Yuri is a sword master. Every one has another special skill. Naoko is able blink into another point in an instance and Yuri can swing her huge sword to push back the enemies around her.

The battle area is not fully develped yet. The size is small, with limited features.

When you first start the game, just input your name and choose your character. Then you can coonect to the game as the host or the client.

Naoko is spawned in the left bottonw corner, while Yuri is in the right top corner. So the game starts right now!
